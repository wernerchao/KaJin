About

Areas

Philip Tong is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist with over 5 years of experience working with individuals, couples, and families. Philip is a big believer in the idea that the strength lies in the client. Philip works from a combination of  solution focused, strength based, and object relations approaches.

 

Philip graduated from the University of Southern California with a Master’s degree in Marriage and Family Therapy (MFT) and with a School Counseling Credential (PPS). During his time at USC, he received the Ruth Shelby Scholarship Award for his academic work.

 

His past experiences include extensive work with children, adolescents, and adults. He has conducted numerous groups and worked with over a hundred different individuals and families during his career.

 

Currently, Philip also works with a non profit organization providing individual and family counseling services for low income families. He is also a member of the California Association of Marriage and Family Therapists (CAMFT).

Treatment Philosophy
