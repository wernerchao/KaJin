About Us

Our Story

Back in college, Jin and Wilson were just two Asians struggling with school life and needed help. There was no easy nor a clear way of finding the right professional guidance they need. In addition, they felt that counseling and therapists were too expensive, inconvenient and stigmatized. The stigmatism is even more severe in Asian than the West. Without proper assistance, Wilson had developed depression and was hospitalized while Jin had anxiety disorder.

A better way...

Jin and Wilson knew that there had to be a better way of finding the right therapy and counseling. Like many people nowadays, the Internet has been a source of help on various topics. Why can’t that be for therapy and counseling?

 

With that question in mind, we launched Kajin Health in late 2015. Kajin Health uses online live video to conduct sessions that are discrete, safe, convenient, affordable and effective. We target Chinese-speaking Asians in particular because they are the least informed and the most stigmatized group for therapy. We chose the name because Kajin sounds like “Happiness” in Chinese.

Within 3 months...

Within 3-4 months of our existence, we have helped over 287+ patients with over 30+ counselors. We are proud of our high caliber counselor/therapists and even prouder of the lives we help improve.

 

As of now, Kajin Health is the first private online therapy and counseling platform in Taiwan and the first to target Asian communities across the world. We continue our mission of helping people and changing people’s lives.

Join us

We are still in infancy however, and we invite you to join us as a counselor/ therapist, team member or some one who wants a happier life.
