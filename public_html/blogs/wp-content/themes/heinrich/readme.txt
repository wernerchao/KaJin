Description: 
Heinrich is a beautiful minimal WordPress theme for your personal blog or portfolio. The theme is fully responsive and looks great on any device. It comes with two widget areas, a social link menu, four post formats (standard, status, link and image) and featured images for posts and pages. Heinrich is translation ready and currently available in English and German.
